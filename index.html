Cookies

Cookies are a way for websites to store data for later visits. This can make users' experiences with sites much more convenient.
(e.g. "Remember me" when logging in).


However, convenience and security are often antithetical. While most websites claim to only use "experience-enhancing" cookies, they often store advertising data to track you across sites, with or without your consent.
